Econometric analysis of production function

Total Tillage explains almost all of the Production. A two-stage least squares method is used, where independent variables predict Total Tillage in the first stage, and the predicted value for Total Tillage is then used to explain Production in the second stage. These estimations confirm that Solow’s model (1957) is the most significant for explaining economic growth in Mato Grosso when considering TFP. More capital per worker, taken as land per worker, leads to higher production.
