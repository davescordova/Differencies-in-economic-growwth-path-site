Components of the production function

  • The functional form can be presented as a Cobb-Douglas one:
    • The Land Market is represented in the sense of capital.
    • Work and other forms of capital, like machines, are represented in their proper sense of labor and capital, respectively.
    • TFP acts as a productivity multiplier for other variables.
    • Human capital depends on the increase of the Working-Age Population (WAP), which is encompassed by labor, leading to Equation 5.

Equation 5 - Functional form

$$ Y = A K^{\alpha} L^{\beta} $$

Legend:

  • $Y$: Total production
  • $A$: TFP
  • $K$: Capital
  • $L$: labor
  • $\alpha, \beta$: coefficients
